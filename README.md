# CANCER-PATIENT-ANALYSIS(Interactive Dashboard Creation Using Power BI)
## Project Objective
To analyze cancer patient data to identify demographic and lifestyle risk patterns, evaluate stage at diagnosis, and generate insights that support early detection and improved healthcare decision-making.
## Dataset Link
https://github.com/shagunkkaushal3-cmd/CANCER-PATIENT-DASHBOARD-/blob/main/colorectal_cancer_prediction.csv
## KPI's
What is the total number of cancer patients in the dataset?
What is the average age of patients diagnosed with cancer?
What is the gender distribution of cancer cases?
Which stage of cancer has the highest number of diagnoses?
What percentage of patients are diagnosed at early stages (Stage I)?
How many patients are current, former, or never smokers?
Which region reports the highest number of cases?
What proportion of patients have insurance coverage?
How does cancer prevalence vary across racial groups?
What percentage of cases are linked to lifestyle risk factors (smoking/alcohol)?
